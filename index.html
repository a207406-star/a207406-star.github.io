Reflection & Learning Journey

My learning curve from Lab 1 to Project 2 has been incredibly transformative. Starting with basic, static Jetpack Compose views, I advanced to complex state handling, full Room structural caching, and asynchronous network operations. The most challenging aspect was managing the lifecycle of on-device sensor hardware while simultaneously updating Firebase cloud entities. Overcoming these limitations taught me the practical side of Android system architecture, and I now feel highly confident building data-driven, reactive mobile applications that address real-world SDG 3 criteria.
